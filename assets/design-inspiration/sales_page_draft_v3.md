You're not the person who can step away to heal.

You have clients on the calendar. A team waiting on decisions. A platform that doesn't pause. You're the face of the business, and the business doesn't run if you're not running.

And somewhere in the middle of all of it, your body started screaming.

Anxiety that won't quit. Panic before launches. Dissociation in the middle of a podcast recording. Symptoms you can't ignore anymore. A creeping certainty that you're one hard week from checking yourself into a psych hospital because you're scared you're about to have a nervous breakdown.

You've tried the obvious things. Therapy. Books. The course your friend swore by. You probably understand the nervous system intellectually. You might be the one teaching it to your own clients.

What you don't know is how to keep building this business without abandoning yourself to do it. Or whether the business you've built is even the one you should be scaling.

That's what this is for.

## WHO THIS IS FOR

This work is for you if:

- You run an online business, have a public-facing brand, or work in a helping profession (therapist, coach, somatic practitioner, healthcare provider)
- Your work involves visibility. You create content, serve clients, or lead in ways that put your name and face out there
- You're still in motion. Still creating, still serving, still shipping
- You're dealing with chronic nervous system symptoms (anxiety, panic, dissociation, somatic stuff, burnout that won't quit) and you're scared of where it's heading
- You don't need someone to convince you that you're safe. You need someone who has been exactly where you are and kept building anyway.

People finding their way to me through this work include a touring musician with stadium-level visibility, a somatic therapist who built her practice and then got sensitized herself, online creators with audiences in the hundreds of thousands, and helping professionals who can name every symptom in their clients but can't seem to fix their own.

If that sounds like you, keep reading.

## WHO THIS IS NOT FOR

I want to be honest about this because the wrong fit doesn't help anyone:

- **This is not sensitization recovery coaching.** If you're looking for help understanding symptoms, getting reassurance about whether you're okay, or working through acute sensitization, my course is built for exactly that. This container is something different.
- **This is not for people who have stepped back from work to heal.** That's a valid path. It's not this path.
- **This is not for people who want daily reassurance or symptom analysis.** WhatsApp support is for the work, not a hotline.
- **This is not for people who aren't ready to make changes inside their business.** The whole premise is that the business itself is part of what's keeping you stuck. If you don't want to look at that, don't apply.

## WHAT WE'LL ACTUALLY DO

This is not a curriculum. It's a custom container, and the work is as much strategic as it is somatic.

Most clients arrive with some version of the same question: how do I keep building this without it costing me my body? Underneath that question is usually a second one they haven't said out loud yet: is this even the business I should be building?

The work moves through some combination of:

**On the business side:**
- Looking at where your business is currently being built from survival, urgency, or self-abandonment, and what changes structurally if you stop
- Auditing offers, pricing, content cadence, team structure, and revenue model through the lens of what's actually sustainable for the version of you running it
- Identifying the specific moves that will let your business make more money with less of you in it
- Building a relationship with visibility, content creation, and public-facing work that doesn't require dissociation to execute on

**On the embodied side:**
- Distinguishing intuition from nervous system noise so you can make decisions you trust
- Recovering self-trust in your creative voice, your offers, your boundaries, and your timing
- Holding the symptoms with enough capacity that they stop running the show

The two sides aren't separate. A business built from a dysregulated nervous system has a ceiling, and you've probably hit it. The work is how you break through it.

## FAQ

**Is this therapy?**
No. I'm not a licensed therapist, and this is not a substitute for mental health care. Many of my clients work with a therapist alongside this container.

**Will you give medical advice?**
No. I don't diagnose, treat, or give medical guidance. If you need medical support, that's a doctor's job.

**How is this different from your course?**
The course teaches the foundational frameworks for understanding sensitization and beginning to recover. This container is for business owners who already have language for what they're experiencing and need help building a business that doesn't require them to abandon themselves to run it.

**What if I just need help with sensitization?**
Take the course. It's the right tool for the job. This isn't.

**Can I pay in installments?**
We can discuss payment structures on the discovery call.

**What if I'm not sure I qualify?**
Apply. The form itself will tell you a lot. If we're not a fit, I'll say so, and I may point you somewhere that is.
